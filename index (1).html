03 — Research

Publications & IP

Published · First Author IF 8.187
IoT-Enabled Smart Home System for Automated Control and Energy Consumption Analysis
IRJMETS · Vol. 07, Issue 10 · October 2025 · e-ISSN: 2582-5208
  • Proposed and implemented a three-layer IoT architecture (Sensing → Control → Cloud) using NodeMCU ESP8266 and PZEM004T sensors; achieved 78% energy measurement efficiency and <1.5 sec Wi-Fi latency.
  • Demonstrated 11–14% reduction in household energy consumption via threshold-based automation logic; system validated across 218–242V, 0.1–2.4A, and 20–540W operating ranges.
  • Built Firebase/Google Sheets cloud data pipeline with real-time HTML/JS dashboard; currently extending with an AI-driven predictive load forecasting model.
  • Published as first author in a peer-reviewed, open-access, fully refereed international journal.

Government of India, Copyright Office · Registered: September 19, 2025 · One of the first formally protected IoT energy management systems by an undergraduate student in India.
